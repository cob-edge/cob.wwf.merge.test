Who We Are

Edge Parking has been curated by us at Team Edge, with the intention of creating a fully autonomous system, designed to take the guess work out of your journey by helping you find a parking location closer to your destination, whilst also safely processing payment transactions. Our technology is designed for everyone, from the daily commuter, all the way to the casual adventurer.
Worry less about the finding where you have to park your vehicle, and more about your day ahead!
